# Legal Compliance & Licensing

Official Portuguese regulatory documents required for project submission to **DGEG** (Directorate-General for Energy and Geology) or the network operator (**E-Redes**).

* **Electrical Data Sheet (Ficha Eletrotécnica):** Defines the supply characteristics (Contracted Power: 320 kVA).
* **Statement of Responsibility (Termo de Responsabilidade):** Formal declaration by the project designer (Tiago Oliveira) ensuring compliance with national safety laws (DL 96/2017).
* **Project Identification:** Official identification of the promoter and the industrial site location.
