# Engineering Calculations & Sizing

This folder contains the mathematical validation for the project, based on an installed power of **400 kVA**.

## Calculation Modules:
1. **Power Balance:** Determination of the utilization power by applying diversity factors (Ks) and utilization factors (Ku) across different sectors.
2. **Cable Sizing:** Selection of cable cross-sections based on voltage drop constraints ($\Delta U$) and current-carrying capacity ($I_z$).
3. **Short-Circuit Study:** Calculation of maximum and minimum $I_{cc}$ to ensure the breaking capacity ($P_{dc}$) of all protection devices (MCBs/MCCBs) is adequate.
